Pre Sales Services

The IT pre-sale is a set of services, actions and procedures that encompass any study, analysis, definition and collection of information prior to sale. Within the technological field, for any IT company, it is fundamental, really necessary and precisely because of what we discussed in the first paragraph of this article: We do not want to sell licenses, but projects.

Objective.

The IT pre-sale aims to know the needs of the client, at all levels, and serves as support to the commercial team so that in this way a project can be presented that covers all the needs of the client, even those that are not contemplated at first, doing this with a predictive analytics that, In addition to giving more credibility to the IT company (provider), it will help minimize the investment in future customer support.

Typical Pre Sales Services.

  • Serve as a link between the IT Inside Sales and the pure commercial team.
  • Presents and give technical support prior implementation of the product or technological solution.
  • Document self-developed IT solutions to streamline commercial interpretation and presentation to the client.
  • Prepare the DEMO, as well as presentations, datasheets or white papers on the solutions.
