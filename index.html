Frequently asked questions

How can I add a consultant to my team? Q

+

The process is straight-forward and simple.
1. You send us the desired skillset and estimated engagement time.
2. We share with you a couple of consultant profiles that meet your needs.
3.You interview them and decide on the best fit.
4. We sign a Statement of Work.
5. An Account Executive schedules regular meetings with you to follow-up on the consultant's performance and to answer any questions.
