What is Customer Relationship Management?

Customer Relationship Management (CRM) is a business strategy that revolves around how a company positions itself in relationship with clients and leads. Traditionally, the concept of CRM involves the relationship within processes of pre-sales (marketing), sales (opportunities), and post-sales (services).

CRM technology has evolved to produce powerful platforms for the complete digitization of customer relationship processes and information. At Tero we work with top technologies to bring our clients the best of what CRM has to offer.
